Our Goal

At Level Ground Learning, we're dedicated to ensuring every child has an equal opportunity to thrive academically. Our goal is to offer free, top-qaulity tutoring to students who need extra support but face barriers to accessing it. By combining personalized mentorship, accessible learning resources, and strong community partnerships, we strive to create an educational environment where every student can succeed, one learner at a time

Important Info

We're a group of passionate high school sophmore from North Jersey who are dedicatd to giving back to those less fortunate through education. Led by Jasmine, Level Ground Learning Offers free acadenic support to kids in our community. Based in Westfield, we hold tutoring sessions at local libraries as well as online. We provide a safe, welcoming, and accessible space for learning. Our sessions will most likely take place on most weekdays from 6:30-8:00 PM
